# Grasshopper

## Ingredients

- 25 ml white crème de cacao
- 25 ml crème de menthe
- 25 ml cream

## Instructions

1. Shake hard with ice; fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Shaved chocolate.
