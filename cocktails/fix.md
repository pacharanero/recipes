# Fix

## Ingredients

- 50 ml base spirit
- 25 ml fresh lemon juice
- 25 ml sweet fruit juice (e.g., pineapple)
- 10 ml gomme syrup

## Instructions

1. Shake hard with ice; strain over fresh ice in Collins glass.

## Garnish

Fruit from recipe.
