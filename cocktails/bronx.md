# Bronx

## Ingredients

- 50 ml dry gin
- 25 ml dry vermouth
- 25 ml sweet vermouth
- 30 ml orange juice
- (Optional) 2 dashes Angostura bitters (Income Tax variation)

## Instructions

1. Shake hard with ice; fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Orange twist.
