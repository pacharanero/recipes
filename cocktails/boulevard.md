# Boulevard

## Ingredients

- 75 ml rye whiskey
- 30 ml dry vermouth
- 15 ml Grand Marnier
- 2 dashes orange bitters

## Instructions

1. Stir with ice until chilled; strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Orange twist and cherry.
