# Last Word

## Ingredients

- 25 ml gin
- 25 ml green Chartreuse
- 25 ml maraschino liqueur
- 25 ml fresh lime juice

## Instructions

1. Shake with ice; fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Lime zest (optional).
