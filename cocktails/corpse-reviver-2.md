# Corpse Reviver #2

## Ingredients

- 25 ml dry gin
- 25 ml triple sec
- 25 ml dry vermouth
- 25 ml fresh lemon juice
- 5 ml absinthe

## Instructions

1. Shake hard with ice; fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Lemon zest.
