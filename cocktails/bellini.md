# Bellini

## Ingredients

- 50 ml white peach purée
- Dry Prosecco to top

## Instructions

1. Add peach purée to chilled sparkling wine glass.
2. Slowly top with Prosecco, gently stirring to integrate without losing effervescence.

## Garnish

None.
