# Sloe Gin Fizz

## Ingredients

- 50 ml sloe gin
- 25 ml fresh lemon juice
- 15 ml sugar syrup
- Soda to top

## Instructions

1. Shake first three with ice; strain into ice-filled highball; top with soda.

## Garnish

Lemon slice.
