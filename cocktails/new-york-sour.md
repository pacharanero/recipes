# New York Sour

## Ingredients

- 50 ml rye or bourbon
- 25 ml fresh lemon juice
- 15 ml sugar syrup
- 10 ml egg white (optional)
- 15 ml fruity red wine (float)

## Instructions

1. Dry shake (if using egg white); add ice; shake again; strain over fresh ice.
2. Float red wine carefully on top.

## Garnish

Lemon twist.
