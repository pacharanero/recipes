# Vesper

## Ingredients

- 45 ml gin
- 15 ml vodka
- 10 ml Lillet Blanc (or dry vermouth)

## Instructions

1. Stir (or shake if Bond) with ice; strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Large lemon zest.
