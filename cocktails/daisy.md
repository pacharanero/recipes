# Daisy

## Ingredients

- 50 ml base spirit (gin, vodka, etc.)
- 25 ml fresh lemon juice
- 10 ml grenadine
- Soda to top

## Instructions

1. Shake first three with ice; strain over fresh ice in Collins glass.
2. Top with soda; gentle stir.

## Garnish

Lime wedge.
