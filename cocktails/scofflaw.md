# Scofflaw

## Ingredients

- 40 ml rye whiskey
- 20 ml dry vermouth
- 15 ml fresh lemon juice
- 15 ml grenadine
- 2 dashes orange bitters

## Instructions

1. Shake with ice; fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Lemon twist.
