# Americano

## Ingredients

- 25 ml Italian sweet vermouth
- 25 ml Campari
- 50 ml soda water (or to top)

## Instructions

1. Add vermouth and Campari to an old fashioned glass filled with ice.
2. Top with chilled soda; give a gentle stir.

## Garnish

Lemon zest and orange slice.
