# Singapore Sling

## Ingredients

- 30 ml gin
- 15 ml cherry brandy
- 7 ml Cointreau
- 7 ml DOM Bénédictine
- 10 ml grenadine
- 50 ml pineapple juice
- 15 ml fresh lime juice
- 1 dash Angostura bitters

## Instructions

1. Shake with ice; strain over fresh ice in tall glass.

## Garnish

Pineapple wedge and cherry.
