# Martini (Dry)

## Ingredients

- 50 ml dry gin
- 15 ml dry vermouth (adjust to taste)
- Optional dash orange bitters

## Instructions

1. Stir ingredients with plenty of ice until very cold.
2. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Lemon twist (express and discard) or olive.
