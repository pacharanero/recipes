# Margarita

## Ingredients

- 40 ml tequila (100% agave)
- 25 ml triple sec
- 20 ml fresh lime juice

## Instructions

1. Shake all ingredients hard with ice.
2. Fine strain into a chilled cocktail or old fashioned glass (salt rim optional beforehand).

## Garnish

Lime wedge.
