# Jungle Bird

## Ingredients

- 40 ml dark rum
- 20 ml Campari
- 30 ml pineapple juice
- 15 ml fresh lime juice
- 10 ml sugar syrup

## Instructions

1. Shake with ice; strain over fresh ice in an old fashioned glass.

## Garnish

Pineapple wedge.
