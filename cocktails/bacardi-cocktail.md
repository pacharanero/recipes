# Bacardi Cocktail

## Ingredients

- 50 ml light rum (Bacardi)
- 25 ml fresh lime juice
- 10 ml grenadine

## Instructions

1. Shake hard with ice; fine strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

None.
