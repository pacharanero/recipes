# Seelbach

## Ingredients

- 25 ml bourbon
- 7 dashes Angostura bitters
- 7 dashes Peychaud's bitters
- 10 ml Cointreau
- Sparkling wine to top

## Instructions

1. Build first three over ice, add Cointreau, brief stir; strain into flute; top sparkling wine gently.

## Garnish

Orange twist.
