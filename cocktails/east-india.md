# East India

## Ingredients

- 75 ml cognac
- 5 ml Grand Marnier
- 5 ml maraschino liqueur
- 5 ml grenadine
- 1 dash Angostura bitters

## Instructions

1. Shake hard with ice; fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Orange twist.
