# Japanese Cocktail

## Ingredients

- 50 ml cognac
- 10 ml orgeat syrup
- 2 dashes Angostura bitters

## Instructions

1. Stir with ice; strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Lemon twist.
