# Gin Gin Mule

## Ingredients

- 50 ml gin
- 15 ml fresh lime juice
- 10 ml sugar syrup
- 2 thin slices fresh ginger
- Ginger beer to top

## Instructions

1. Muddle ginger in shaker; add remaining ingredients (except ginger beer) and ice; shake.
2. Strain into ice-filled Collins glass; top with ginger beer.

## Garnish

Lime wedge.
