# Harvard

## Ingredients

- 40 ml cognac
- 50 ml sweet vermouth
- 2 dashes orange bitters (optional)
- Soda to top (optional)

## Instructions

1. Stir cognac, vermouth, bitters with ice; strain into cocktail glass (or over ice then top soda).

## Garnish

Lemon twist.
