# Paper Plane

## Ingredients

- 25 ml bourbon
- 25 ml Aperol
- 25 ml Amaro Nonino (sub Montenegro)
- 25 ml fresh lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Shake with ice; fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Lemon twist (optional).
