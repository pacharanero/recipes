# Long Island Iced Tea

## Ingredients

- 10 ml vodka
- 10 ml gin
- 10 ml white rum
- 10 ml tequila
- 10 ml triple sec
- 20 ml fresh lemon juice
- 10 ml sugar syrup
- Cola to top

## Instructions

1. Build over ice in highball; brief shake or stir; top with cola.

## Garnish

Lemon wedge.
