# Champs-Elysées

## Ingredients

- 40 ml cognac
- 12.5 ml green Chartreuse
- 12.5 ml fresh lemon juice
- 5 ml simple syrup
- 2 dashes Angostura bitters

## Instructions

1. Shake hard with ice; fine strain into cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Lemon zest.
