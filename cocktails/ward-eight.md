# Ward Eight

## Ingredients

- 40 ml rye whiskey
- 15 ml fresh lemon juice
- 15 ml fresh orange juice
- 15 ml grenadine

## Instructions

1. Shake with ice; fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Cherry.
