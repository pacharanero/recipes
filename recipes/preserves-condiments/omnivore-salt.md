# Omnivore-Style Seasoning Salt

A homemade take on Angelo Garro's Omnivore Salt: a simple, versatile seasoning and finishing salt of sea salt, black pepper, red pepper and fennel.

## Ingredients

- 100 g good-quality coarse sea salt
- 1 tbsp freshly ground black pepper
- ½-1 tbsp crushed red pepper (adjust heat to taste)
- 1-2 tbsp dried fennel fronds, or finely chopped fennel seeds

## Instructions

1. Lightly toast the fennel seeds in a dry pan, if using, then crush them.
2. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl until evenly combined.
3. Taste and adjust: add more fennel for sweeter, herbal notes, or more red pepper for heat.
4. For a finer, more integrated texture, grind together in a mortar and pestle or spice grinder.
5. Store in an airtight jar or shaker.
