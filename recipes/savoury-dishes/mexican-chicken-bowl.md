# Mexican Chicken Bowl

## Ingredients

- Lettuce - 100 g, chopped
- Brown rice (microwaveable) - 100 g
- Chicken breast, skinless, grilled - 1 medium, chopped
- Black beans in water - 1/2 can, drained
- Fresh salsa (no oil) - 2 tbsp (pico de gallo)
- Fresh guacamole (no oil) - 1 tbsp
- Half-fat Cheddar cheese - 1 tbsp, grated
