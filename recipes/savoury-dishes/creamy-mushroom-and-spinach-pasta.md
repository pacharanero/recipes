# Creamy Mushroom & Spinach Pasta

## Ingredients

- Cooking spray
- 1 medium onion, finely sliced
- 2 garlic cloves, crushed
- 400 g mushrooms, roughly chopped
- 100 g Philadelphia Light soft cheese
- 1/2 vegetable stock cube, made up to 150 ml
- 150 g spinach
- 250 g wholewheat pasta

## Instructions

1. Lightly mist a deep frying pan with cooking spray and cook the onion over a medium heat for 4-5 minutes until softened. Add the garlic, season well with salt and black pepper, then cook 1 minute.
2. Add the mushrooms with a splash of water and turn the heat up slightly. Cook 4-5 minutes, stirring often, until golden.
3. Reduce the heat, add the soft cheese and stir until it melts. Pour in the stock, season with black pepper and cook 8-10 minutes until slightly thickened.
4. Cook the pasta according to pack instructions. Drain well and stir through the spinach to wilt.
5. Toss the sauce with the pasta and serve.
