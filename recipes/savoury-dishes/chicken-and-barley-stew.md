# Chicken and Barley Stew

## Ingredients

- 1 vegetable stock cube, made up to 400 ml
- 1 bay leaf
- 20 g pearl barley, washed
- 1 celery stick, chopped
- 1 medium carrot, chopped
- 1 medium leek, trimmed and chopped
- 120 g chicken breast, grilled
- 1 tsp fresh chives
