# Chicken, Broccoli & Tortellini Soup

## Ingredients

- 500 ml fresh chicken stock
- 1 medium carrot, diced
- 1 garlic clove, crushed
- 50 g broccoli florets
- 50 g four-cheese tortelloni
- 80 g chicken breast, grilled and chopped
- 1 large tomato, diced
- 1 1/2 tbsp Parmesan, grated
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
