# Cooking Eggs in an Air Fryer

Air fryers vary, so treat these timings as starting points and adjust after the first batch.

## Whole Eggs in the Shell

Use this method for boiled-style eggs.

### Ingredients

- Eggs, in their shells
- Ice and cold water, for cooling

### Instructions

1. Place the eggs directly in the air fryer basket.
2. Cook at 130–150°C until done to your liking:
   - Soft-boiled style: 10–11 minutes
   - Hard-boiled style: about 15 minutes
3. Transfer the eggs straight into iced water to stop the cooking.
4. Peel once cool enough to handle.

## Fried Eggs

### Ingredients

- 1 egg
- A little oil or butter, for greasing

### Instructions

1. Lightly grease a small oven-safe dish, ramekin, or silicone mould.
2. Crack in the egg.
3. Air fry at 180°C for 3–5 minutes, until the white is set and the yolk is done to your liking.

## Scrambled Eggs

### Ingredients

- 1–2 eggs
- A little oil or butter, for greasing
- Salt and pepper, to taste

### Instructions

1. Lightly grease a ramekin or small oven-safe dish.
2. Whisk the eggs with salt and pepper.
3. Air fry at 180°C, stirring once or twice, until just set.

## Poached-Style Egg

This is not the same as a traditional poached egg, but it gives a soft egg cooked in water.

### Ingredients

- 1 egg
- A little water

### Instructions

1. Add a shallow layer of water to a ramekin.
2. Crack in the egg.
3. Cover the ramekin with foil.
4. Air fry at 180°C until the white is set and the yolk is done to your liking.
