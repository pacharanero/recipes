# Healthy Rhubarb Flapjacks

Oat bars layered with a fresh orange and rhubarb compote, sweetened with banana, dates and honey rather than refined sugar.

## Ingredients

### Compote

- 2 oranges
- 3 sticks rhubarb
- 1 inch (about 2.5 cm) fresh ginger, grated

### Flapjack

- 150 g oats
- 100 g dates, blitzed in a blender with a splash of water
- 50 g pumpkin seeds
- 50 g sunflower seeds
- 2 bananas, mashed
- 3 tbsp honey
- About 10 almonds, chopped

## Instructions

1. Make the compote: chop the oranges and rhubarb, add the grated ginger, and cook gently in a pan until reduced down.
2. Heat the oven to gas mark 5 (190°C / 375°F).
3. In a bowl, mix the oats, blitzed dates, pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, mashed bananas, honey and chopped almonds.
4. Press half the oat mixture into a greased, lined tin.
5. Spread the rhubarb compote over the base, then top with the remaining oat mixture like a sandwich.
6. Bake for about 45 minutes.
7. Cool before slicing.
