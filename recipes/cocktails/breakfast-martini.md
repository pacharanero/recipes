# Breakfast Martini

## Ingredients

- 50 ml gin
- 12.5 ml triple sec
- 12.5 ml fresh lemon juice
- 1 bar spoon orange marmalade

## Instructions

1. Stir marmalade with other liquids briefly to dissolve.
2. Add ice; shake hard; fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Orange twist.
