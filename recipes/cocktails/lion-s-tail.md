# Lion's Tail

## Ingredients

- 50 ml bourbon
- 15 ml allspice dram
- 15 ml fresh lime juice
- 10 ml sugar syrup
- 2 dashes Angostura bitters

## Instructions

1. Shake with ice; fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Lime twist.
