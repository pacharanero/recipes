# Fino Highball

## Ingredients

- 50 ml gin
- 50 ml fino sherry
- 12.5 ml lime juice
- 2 tsp sugar syrup
- 50 ml orange juice
- Tonic water, to top

## Instructions

1. Add all ingredients except tonic water to a cocktail shaker with ice and shake.
2. Pour into a highball glass filled with ice.
3. Top with tonic water.

## Garnish

Lemon wedge.
