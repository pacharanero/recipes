# Irish Coffee

## Ingredients

- 40 ml Irish whiskey
- 75 ml strong hot coffee
- 10 ml demerara sugar syrup
- Lightly whipped double cream (float)

## Instructions

1. Warm glass. Add whiskey, hot coffee, syrup; stir.
2. Float cream gently over back of spoon.

## Garnish

Freshly grated nutmeg (optional).
