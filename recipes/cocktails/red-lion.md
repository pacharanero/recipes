# Red Lion

## Ingredients

- 25 ml gin
- 25 ml Grand Marnier (or triple sec)
- 15 ml fresh orange juice
- 15 ml fresh lemon juice
- 5 ml grenadine

## Instructions

1. Shake with ice; fine strain into cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Orange twist.
