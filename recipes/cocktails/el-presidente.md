# El Presidente

## Ingredients

- 50 ml white rum
- 25 ml pineapple juice
- 20 ml fresh lime juice
- 10 ml grenadine

## Instructions

1. Shake hard with ice; strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Lime wedge.
