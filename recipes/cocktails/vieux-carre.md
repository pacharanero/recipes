# Vieux Carré

## Ingredients

- 30 ml rye whiskey
- 30 ml cognac
- 30 ml sweet vermouth
- 5 ml DOM Bénédictine
- 2 dashes Peychaud's bitters
- 2 dashes Angostura bitters

## Instructions

1. Stir with ice; strain into rocks glass over fresh ice.

## Garnish

Lemon twist.
