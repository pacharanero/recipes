# White Lady

## Ingredients

- 40 ml gin
- 20 ml triple sec
- 20 ml fresh lemon juice
- 10 ml egg white (optional)

## Instructions

1. Dry shake (if using egg white); add ice; shake hard; fine strain.

## Garnish

Lemon zest.
