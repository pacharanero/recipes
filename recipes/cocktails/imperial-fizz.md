# Imperial Fizz

## Ingredients

- 50 ml gin
- 25 ml fresh lemon juice
- 15 ml sugar syrup
- Sparkling wine to top

## Instructions

1. Shake first three with ice; strain into flute; top with sparkling wine.

## Garnish

Lemon twist.
