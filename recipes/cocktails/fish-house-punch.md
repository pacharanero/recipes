# Fish House Punch

> Classic punch; scale quantities proportionally for bowl service.

## Ingredients

- 25 ml brandy
- 25 ml gold rum
- 20 ml peach liqueur
- 20 ml fresh lemon juice
- 10 ml gomme syrup
- Soda to top

## Instructions

1. Shake first five with ice; fine strain into ice-filled glass; top with soda.

## Garnish

Grated nutmeg and lemon slice.
