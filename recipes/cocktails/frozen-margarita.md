# Frozen Margarita

## Ingredients

- 50ml tequila
- 25ml triple sec (e.g. Cointreau)
- 25ml lime juice
- 15ml sugar syrup
- large handful of ice
- wedge of lime, to garnish

## Instructions

1. Put all the ingredients except the lime wedge in a blender and blitz until smooth.
2. Tip into a cocktail, margarita, or rocks glass.
3. Garnish with the lime wedge and serve immediately.
