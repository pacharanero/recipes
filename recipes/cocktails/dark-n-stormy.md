# Dark n Stormy

## Ingredients

- 50 ml Goslings Black Seal rum
- 25 ml fresh lime juice
- Ginger beer to top

## Instructions

1. Build over ice in a Collins glass; top with ginger beer; brief stir.

## Garnish

Lime wedge.
