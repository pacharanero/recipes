# Blood and Sand

## Ingredients

- 20 ml Scotch whisky
- 20 ml cherry brandy
- 20 ml sweet vermouth
- 20 ml orange juice

## Instructions

1. Shake with ice; fine strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Orange zest.
