# Hanky-Panky Martini

> Ada Coleman, American Bar, Savoy Hotel.

## Ingredients

- 50 ml dry gin
- 50 ml sweet vermouth
- 5 ml Fernet Branca
- 10 ml orange juice

## Instructions

1. Stir with ice; strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Orange twist.
