# Piña Colada

## Ingredients

- 50 ml white rum
- 50 ml pineapple juice
- 25 ml cream of coconut
- 25 ml coconut milk (optional for texture)

## Instructions

1. Blend with crushed ice until smooth (or shake hard with ice and strain over crushed ice).

## Garnish

Pineapple slice and cherry.
