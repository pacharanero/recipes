# Cosmopolitan

## Ingredients

- 40 ml lemon vodka (tequila for Rude Cosmo)
- 20 ml triple sec
- 15 ml fresh lime juice
- 30 ml cranberry juice
- Dash orange bitters

## Instructions

1. Shake hard with ice; fine strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Flamed orange zest.
