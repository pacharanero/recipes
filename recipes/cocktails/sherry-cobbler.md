# Sherry Cobbler

## Ingredients

- 75 ml amontillado or oloroso sherry
- 10 ml sugar syrup
- 2 orange slices
- 2 berries (seasonal)

## Instructions

1. Muddle fruit with syrup; add sherry and crushed ice; churn; top with more crushed ice.

## Garnish

Seasonal berries and mint sprig.
