# Gin Cocktail

## Ingredients

- 75 ml gin
- 5 ml triple sec
- 5 ml gomme syrup
- 2 dashes Angostura bitters

## Instructions

1. Stir with ice; strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Lemon zest.
