# Toronto

## Ingredients

- 50 ml Canadian or rye whisky
- 15 ml Fernet Branca
- 10 ml sugar syrup
- 2 dashes Angostura bitters

## Instructions

1. Stir with ice; strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Orange twist.
