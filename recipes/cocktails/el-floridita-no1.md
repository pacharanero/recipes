# El Floridita No. 1

## Ingredients

- 50 ml rum
- 25 ml fresh lime juice
- 10 ml gomme syrup
- 10 ml maraschino liqueur

## Instructions

1. Shake hard with ice; fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

None.
