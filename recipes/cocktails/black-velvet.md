# Black Velvet

## Ingredients

- 100 ml chilled Guinness stout
- Champagne to top

## Instructions

1. Pour stout gently into a chilled flute.
2. Slowly top with Champagne over the back of a spoon to layer (optional) or simply top.

## Garnish

None.
