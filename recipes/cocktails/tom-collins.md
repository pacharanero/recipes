# Tom Collins

## Ingredients

- 50 ml gin
- 25 ml fresh lemon juice
- 15 ml sugar syrup
- Soda to top

## Instructions

1. Build over ice in Collins glass; gentle stir.

## Garnish

Lemon slice and cherry.
