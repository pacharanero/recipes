# Hemingway Special

## Ingredients

- 50 ml white rum
- 30 ml fresh grapefruit juice
- 15 ml fresh lime juice
- 10 ml maraschino liqueur
- 5 ml sugar syrup (optional to taste)

## Instructions

1. Shake with ice; fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Grapefruit zest.
