# Sherry Flip

## Ingredients

- 60 ml oloroso or amontillado sherry
- 10 ml sugar syrup
- 1 whole egg
- 1 dash Angostura bitters

## Instructions

1. Dry shake; add ice; shake hard; fine strain into small stemmed glass.

## Garnish

Grated nutmeg.
