# Clover Club

## Ingredients

- 50 ml dry gin
- 25 ml fresh lemon juice
- 10 ml raspberry syrup
- 10 ml egg white

## Instructions

1. Dry shake first; add ice and shake hard.
2. Fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

None (optional raspberry).
