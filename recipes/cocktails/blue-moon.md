# Blue Moon

## Ingredients

- 50 ml dry gin
- 25 ml fresh lemon juice
- 20 ml crème Yvette or crème de violette
- (Optional) 10 ml egg white

## Instructions

1. (If using egg white) Dry shake; add ice and shake hard.
2. Fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Raspberry.
