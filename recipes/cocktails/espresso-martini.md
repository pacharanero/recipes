# Espresso Martini

## Ingredients

- 25 ml vodka
- 25 ml coffee liqueur
- 25 ml fresh espresso (chilled)

## Instructions

1. Shake very hard with ice for crema; fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Three coffee beans.
