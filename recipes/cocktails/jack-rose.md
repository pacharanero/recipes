# Jack Rose

## Ingredients

- 50 ml applejack (or calvados)
- 25 ml fresh lemon or lime juice
- 15 ml grenadine

## Instructions

1. Shake with ice; fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Lemon or lime twist.
