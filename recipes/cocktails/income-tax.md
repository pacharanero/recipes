# Income Tax Cocktail

## Ingredients

- 40 ml dry gin
- 20 ml dry vermouth
- 20 ml sweet vermouth
- 20 ml fresh orange juice
- 2 dashes Angostura bitters

## Instructions

1. Shake with ice; fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Orange twist.
