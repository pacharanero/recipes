# Painkiller

## Ingredients

- 50 ml dark rum
- 75 ml pineapple juice
- 25 ml orange juice
- 25 ml cream of coconut

## Instructions

1. Shake with ice; strain over fresh crushed ice in large glass.

## Garnish

Grated nutmeg and pineapple wedge.
