# French 75

## Ingredients

- 35 ml gin
- 15 ml fresh lemon juice
- 5 ml gomme syrup
- Sparkling wine to top

## Instructions

1. Shake first three with ice; fine strain into flute; top with sparkling wine.

## Garnish

Lemon zest.
