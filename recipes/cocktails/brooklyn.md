# Brooklyn

## Ingredients

- 75 ml bourbon
- 15 ml dry vermouth
- 15 ml sweet vermouth
- 10 ml maraschino liqueur
- 3 dashes Angostura bitters

## Instructions

1. Stir with ice; strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Maraschino cherry.
