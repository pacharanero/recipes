# Diplomat

## Ingredients

- 50 ml dry vermouth
- 25 ml sweet vermouth
- 5 ml maraschino liqueur
- 2 dashes orange bitters

## Instructions

1. Stir all ingredients with ice; strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

Orange twist.
