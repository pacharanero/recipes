# Horse's Neck

## Ingredients

- 50 ml brandy (or bourbon)
- Ginger ale to top
- 2 dashes Angostura bitters (optional)

## Instructions

1. Build over ice in highball glass; add bitters if using; top ginger ale and gently stir.

## Garnish

Long lemon peel spiral.
