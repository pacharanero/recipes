# Naked & Famous

## Ingredients

- 25 ml mezcal
- 25 ml yellow Chartreuse
- 25 ml Aperol
- 25 ml fresh lime juice

## Instructions

1. Shake with ice; fine strain into chilled cocktail glass.

## Garnish

None.
