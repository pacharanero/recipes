# Pisco Sour

## Ingredients

- 50 ml pisco
- 25 ml fresh lime juice
- 15 ml sugar syrup
- 15 ml egg white
- 2 dashes Angostura or Amargo Chuncho bitters

## Instructions

1. Dry shake; add ice; shake hard; fine strain into coupe.

## Garnish

Bitters dashed art on foam.
